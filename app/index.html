About Ali + Moof

When we met while working on the same-sex marriage campaign in Maine in 2012, we were deeply in love . . . Moof with Los Angeles, and Ali with Maine. Neither of us would have predicted that three years and many plane tickets later, we would be living together in a new city, planning a wedding that will now be recognized in all fifty states! But here we are, and our life together is just as joyous now as it was unimaginable then. Together, we've learned to love hiking and running, and we've taken on the task of eating all the tastiest food in PDX. Ali appreciates Moof's excellent fashion taste and technological know-how and Moof benefits from Ali's green thumb, knack for cooking, and spider-killing skills. What more could a couple need to be happy?! Just our amazing friends and loving families. Thank you for being part of our wedding: we can't wait to celebrate our love and commitment to each other with all of you!

Ceremony + Reception

We are so happy that our friends and family will be gathering in Laguna Beach, California, to celebrate our love. The ceremony and reception will be held at the Surf & Sand Resort, a beautiful hotel right on the beach, about a 20 minute drive from where Moof grew up.

  • 6:30 pm Ceremony on the Ocean Terrace
  • 7:00 pm Cocktails on the Sand Dollar Terrace
  • 7:45 pm Dinner in the Sand Dollar Ballroom
  • 9:00 pm Lantern lighting on the beach

Parking for guests is in the Surf & Sand's valet parking lot (free for wedding guests). Be careful turning off the Pacific Coast Highway, the driveway is steep!

We love your kids, and so children are welcome for all parts of the evening. And if you'd like, we will also have a babysitter on site. Please contact Ali if you're interested in child care so we can make arrangements.

Surf & Sand Resort

This is where the wedding will be. We have set aside a small number of rooms for guests in this luxury hotel ($350 a night plus taxes and fees.) 855-690-7114

Best Western Plus Laguna Brisas Spa Hotel

We have a block of rooms for guests at this charming hotel, only steps away from the Surf & Sand Resort and the beach ($250 a night, plus taxes and fees). This is the perfect place to stay if you want an easy walk to and from the wedding. 949-497-7272

The Inn At Laguna Beach

This luxurious modern hotel is a little over a mile from the wedding location, and we have a block of rooms for guests here ($270 a night). A great way to turn the wedding into a weekend getaway if you're coming from the Los Angeles area, or further! 800-544-4479

14 West Laguna Beach

This unique self-service hotel offers 14 studio-style rooms with a kitchenette and living area, making it a good choice for families or if you're planning a longer stay in Laguna Beach. It is about a half mile from the wedding location. We don't have a reserved block of rooms, so just call them if you're interested in making a reservation (rooms are in the low-to-mid $200s). 877-318-1552
